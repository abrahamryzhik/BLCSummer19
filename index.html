Welcome to BLC Tutorials

On this page you will find tutorials to help you replicate the work done by John Coyle and Abraham Ryzhik at the Bill Lane Center in the Summer of 2019 while investigating trends in EPA violations nationally. Currently, the main tutorials we have describe how to scrape data from the facility reports pages given by the EPA and how to use GIS (Geographic Information System) to make maps of this data.
